Frequently Asked Questions

Will my subscription automatically renew next year?

Yes, unless you cancel. You can cancel the subscription at any time during the year within your account page. We'll also send reminders about upcoming renewal 7 days beforehand, which allows the cancellation of an unwanted subscription.

What happens after my subscription expires?

You can continue to use any software you have already downloaded. The usage license never expires. After your subscription expires, you will not be able to access the software downloads. You will not have access to new software updates, new products, or technical support services.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription any time. There is no contract. If you cancel half way through the year, your subscription will continue to be active until the end of the year, and then it will expire.

Does HWDMediaShare have server requirements?

Yes, there are server requirements that go beyond those of Joomla.

Can I get a refund?

Yes, if you're not satisfied with HWD MediaShare when you first subscribe, we'll be happy to cancel your account and offer you a refund within 15 days of purchase.

If I subscribe to HWDMediaShare, will I be able to download new updates?

When you subscription is active, you can access all software updates. If your subscription expires, and you wish you to continue downloading updates, you will need to renew your subscription.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Visa, Visa Electron, Visa Debit, Maestro, Mastercard, Solo and PayPal.

Is your source code encoded?

No. Our software is not encoded. You have access to all the source code, and you have permission to make modifications.

Will I be charged VAT?

Value Added Tax (VAT) will be applied to sales within the EU. Sales outside the EU will not be charged any tax.

Will my license ever expire?

No. The usage license will never expire.
